        <article class="project-card">
          <img src="cograsper.jpg" class="project-image" alt="Co-grasper prosthetic hand">

          <h3>Co-grasper</h3>

          <p>
            The development of a co-grasping hybrid prosthetic hand—where human force (via bowen cable) and robotic force (via motor) combine to independently and simultaneously act on a single gripper.
          </p>

          <div class="tags">
            <span>Design</span>
            <span>Fabrication</span>
            <span>Validation</span>
            <span>Human subject testing</span>
          </div>
        </article>

        <article class="project-card">
          <img src="vcvo.jpg" class="project-image" alt="Voluntary-open and voluntary-close prosthetic terminal device">

          <h3>Adjustable Force Transmission in Voluntary-Open and Voluntary-Close Prosthetic Terminal Devices</h3>

          <p>
            This design preserves consistent cable excursion characteristics across both modes and incorporates an adjustable spring mechanism that allows the restoring force to be tuned without hardware modification. Together, these features aim to improve usability across varied grasping tasks.
          </p>

          <div class="tags">
            <span>Design</span>
